Biography

He Li received the BS and MS degrees from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2007 and 2009, respectively. He received Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China. Currently, he is a postdoctoral fellow in with Department of Information and Electronic Engineering, Muroran Institute of Technology, Japan.His research interests include cloud computing and software defined networking. He is a student member of the IEEE and the IEEE Communication Society.
