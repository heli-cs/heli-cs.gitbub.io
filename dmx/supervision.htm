Research Opportunities ...

Opportunity for PhD study with full scholarship available at the School of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (http://www.ntu.edu.sg/EEE/). Research directions include (but not limited to): Big Data Security and Privacy; Smart Phone Security; Machine to Machine Communication Security. Requirements include: (1) Bachelor or Master's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Applied Math or a related discipline; (2) High GPA; (3) Good programming and analysis skills; and (4) Good TOEFL and GRE scores. Interested students please send a brief CV and unofficial transcript to: rxlu@ntu.edu.sg
